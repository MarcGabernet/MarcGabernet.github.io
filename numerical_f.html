Numerical Factory

Virtual reality project made in collaboration with Numerical Factory, a UPC's mathematics department initiative. This project was made using Unity and explores how to set up a VR experience and records the steps to follow to learn.
Aside from the basics of VR, a full project was also developed: a simulation of a laparoscopy , a medica procedure, that explores the physics related problem of how to simulate a soft body object and the interactions with the laparoscopic tools.
